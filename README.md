# CALAMITY

*You don't play a monster — you play an apocalypse.*

2D side-on arcade destruction. Pick one of three mythic calamities, pick a city,
and raze it for score before the army buries you. Runs open at dusk and end in
fire — or in a night you made permanent.

![Godot 4.7](https://img.shields.io/badge/Godot-4.7-478cbf) ![status](https://img.shields.io/badge/status-prototype-orange)

## The calamities

| | verb | special |
|---|---|---|
| **THE SWARM** | mouse-aimed tendrils — chew buildings, snatch crowds, reel vehicles in and crush them | evolution tree: IRONMAW mauls / GOREHOOK slings / SPORE BLOOM pods |
| **KERAUNOS** | colossal storm hydra — banked lightning strikes at the cursor | TEMPEST barrage; branches: MANYHEADS / BALL LIGHTNING / SKYFALL |
| **TZITZIMITL** | plumed serpent that flies at your cursor — lance dives pierce whole buildings | **devours the sun** — permanent darkness, blind armies, dead lights |

## The cities

- **NEW KOWLOON** — neon megacity (art: Warped City)
- **THORNSPIRE** — gothic spires under an aurora, hardened garrison (art: GothicVania)
- **ASHPORT** — rusting industrial sprawl, endless reinforcements (art: Industrial Parallax)

Everything is destructible: buildings (pixel-carved wounds, fire that spreads),
street lamps, cars, billboards, control boxes — and the crowds, dogs, pigeons
and pigs that flee you.

## Controls

WASD / arrows — move · LMB — attack at cursor · RMB — special / evolved skill
· R — restart · ESC — menu

## Run from source

```
Godot 4.7 --path .
```
